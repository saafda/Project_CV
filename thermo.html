Thermodynamic irreversibility

Studying thermodynamic irreversibility using two dog model

In this project the goal was to study thermodynamic irreversibility using two dogs with flees on them as model. One dog starts by having the entire flee population of one on itself then for each time-unit a random jumps from one dog to the other.
