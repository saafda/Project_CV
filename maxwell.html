Maxwell-Boltzmann Velocity Distribution

Studying the Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution with disks

The goal of this project was to use light plastic disks on a constantly moving air hockey table with soft strings as walls to simulate particles moving in a gas. First I had to record the movement and position of the moving disks using a tracker program. Then using the positional data from the tracker I was able to calculate the velocity distribution of the simulated gas and saw that it seemed to follow the Maxwell-Boltzmann-distribution.
