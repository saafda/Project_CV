Summary

  • Associate professor (Maître de Conférences, since 2021) at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF), Valenciennes, France.
  • Postdoctoral researcher (2020-2021) at the French Aerospace Lab ONERA (Toulouse), France. Research project: Design of experiments and surrogate models for aerodynamic data.
  • Postdoctoral researcher (2019-2020) at IMT and BRGM, France. ANR Research project: Risk-based system for coastal flooding early warning (RISCOPE).
  • Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics (2016-2019) at Mines de Saint-Étienne, France. I worked under the supervision of Prof. Olivier Roustant, Ph.D. Nicolas Durrande, and Ph.D. François Bachoc. The thesis was funded by the Chair in Applied Mathematics OQUAIDO [activity report]
  • M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (2013-2015) at Universidad Tecnólogica de Pereira, Colombia. I worked under the supervision of Ph.D. Mauricio Álvarez.
  • My research interests include applied mathematics, machine learning and computer science topics. More precisely, Gaussian processes, kernel design and surrogate probabilistic models.
